An Escape Even the Eldar Would Envy

Tucked into the silver coastline of the Gulf of Lhûn, where the Ered Luin catch the last light of evening, the Grey Havens Resort & Spa offers a seaside sanctuary far from the noise of the Third Age. Our shores are white, our tides are gentle, and our staff have been welcoming weary travellers — hobbits, rangers, and the occasional wizard — for over an Age and a half.

Whether you've come to rest after a long road, celebrate a hard-won victory, or simply watch swan-ships glide toward the horizon, there is a room, a bench, and a warm drink waiting for you.
